Privacy Policy

Last updated: YYYY-MM-DD

1. Information We Collect

This extension does not collect, transmit or store any personal data. All settings (particle count, link distance, etc.) are saved locally in your browser’s chrome.storage.sync area and are never shared with any server.

2. Remote Resources

We load optional configuration modules and theme files from our secure HTTPS server to allow real-time visual updates without republishing the extension. No user-identifying information is sent alongside these requests.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate analytics, advertising, or any other third-party trackers.

4. Data Security

All remote assets are served over HTTPS and integrity-checked via Subresource Integrity (SRI). We follow Chrome’s best practices for extension security.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. If we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at you@yourdomain.com.
